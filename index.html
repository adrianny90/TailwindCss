Founding story

Our plant shop blossomed from a humble little apartment jungle I began cultivating during a particularly dreary, rain-soaked summer back in 2018. What started as a personal passion project—born out of an almost compulsive need to rescue drooping, wilting houseplants from their sad fates—slowly grew into something much bigger: a heartfelt dream to spread that vibrant, green magic far beyond my own walls. It all began with a few neglected pots, a bit of stubborn determination, and a lot of trial and error as I learned how to coax life back into those leafy companions. Over time, that tiny indoor oasis became a thriving haven, and I realized I wanted others to experience the same joy and connection that plants brought me. Today, we’re a small but passionate team, united by that same love for greenery and a shared mission to bring a touch of nature into every home, office, or quiet corner of your life. We’ve traded my cramped apartment for a proper space, but the heart of it all remains unchanged: finding the perfect plant to suit your space, your style, and your story. Whether it’s a sturdy succulent for a sunny windowsill, a lush fern to breathe life into a shady nook, or a quirky cactus for a minimalist vibe, we’re here to help you discover the ideal green companion. Our journey started with a single rainy season and a handful of rescued plants, and now it’s about growing alongside our community, one pot at a time.

What is actually our mission?

"Our mission is to weave the breathtaking beauty of nature into the fabric of every home, delivering that magic one plant at a time. We’re driven by a deep-rooted passion to empower people to cultivate their own lush, green sanctuaries—personal havens that bloom with life, tranquility, and a touch of the wild. It’s not just about selling plants; it’s about fostering a connection between people and the natural world, helping them discover the joy of nurturing something living and watching it flourish. We believe every leaf, every stem, carries the potential to transform a space and uplift a spirit. Our commitment goes beyond aesthetics—we’re devoted to curating a collection of sustainable, thoughtfully sourced plants that are as kind to the planet as they are to the homes they grace. Each one is selected with care, ensuring it’s not only beautiful but also resilient, ready to thrive under the right conditions and inspire those who tend to it. From vibrant, low-maintenance beauties to delicate growers that reward attentive hands, we’re here to guide you in building a green retreat that reflects your personality and lifestyle. With every pot we place into your hands, we aim to spark a little wonder, a lot of growth, and a lasting bond with the natural world."
