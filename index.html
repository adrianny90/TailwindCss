Founding Story

Our plant shop sprouted from a tiny apartment jungle I started during a rainy summer in 2018. What began as a personal obsession with rescuing wilting houseplants turned into a dream to share that green magic with others. Today, we’re a small team dedicated to finding the perfect plant for every corner of your life.

What is actually our mission?

"Our mission is to bring nature’s beauty into every home, one plant at a time. We’re passionate about helping people grow their own green sanctuaries with sustainable, lovingly curated plants that thrive and inspire."
